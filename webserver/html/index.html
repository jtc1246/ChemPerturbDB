ChemicalDB

Welcome to chemicalDB!

ChemicalDB contains single cell RNA sequencing (scRNAseq) data from the EndoC-βH1 cell line, an immortalized human β-cell line, treated with 46 different hormones from our in-house hormone screening library. We incorporate 10-bp unique barcodes into the EndoC-βH1 line via lentiviral infection and treat each unique barcoded line with a single hormone from our screening library for 48 hours. Libraries were prepared using the Chromium Single Cell 3' v3.1 Chemistry Dual Index platform with Feature Barcoding technology for Cell Multiplexing and sequenced at a depth of 20,000 read pairs per cell. Users are able to analyze the expression of genes of treated cells normalized to their respective controls, visualize the activation/suppression of major pathways, perform geneset enrichment analysis (GSEA) of custom genesets, as well as perform correlation analysis of custom genesets via this platform. More information and tutorials regarding the usage of different modules can be found in the Help section.
